Specialized Directorates

Mulago operates twelve specialist institutes, each with dedicated consultants, theatres, and wards. Find the right institute for your care.

Uganda Heart Institute

Comprehensive cardiac care including interventional cardiology, cardiac surgery, and echocardiography. Uganda's only public cardiac catheterization lab.

Cardiology Block A +256 414 541 900
🎗️

Uganda Cancer Institute

Uganda's sole public cancer treatment centre offering chemotherapy, radiotherapy, and palliative services. Free services for eligible patients.

Oncology Block B +256 414 541 910
🧠

Neurosciences Unit

Diagnosis and management of stroke, epilepsy, brain tumours, spinal cord injuries, and other neurological conditions.

Neurology Block E +256 414 541 920
🍼

Neonatal & Paediatric Unit

Specialist care for newborns and children including NICU, paediatric surgery, and child nutrition programmes.

Paediatrics Block C +256 414 541 930

Reproductive Health Unit

Orthopaedic & Trauma Centre

Bone and joint surgery, trauma care, physiotherapy, and rehabilitation. Uganda's main trauma referral point.

Orthopaedics Block D +256 414 541 950

Uganda Eye Centre

Cataract surgery, glaucoma management, diabetic retinopathy, corneal disease, and low-vision services.

Ophthalmology Block G +256 414 541 960

Pathology & Laboratory

Full clinical pathology services including haematology, microbiology, histopathology, and molecular diagnostics.

Digital Intake

Skip the Paper Queue

No more traveling to Mulago just to join a queue and be told to come back tomorrow. Submit your appointment request digitally — we'll confirm via SMS within 24 hours.

  • 1
    Fill the Form Enter your name, National ID (NIN), and reason for visit.
  • 2
    Get a Reference Code You receive a unique booking reference code immediately.
  • 3
    SMS Confirmation Hospital admin confirms your date and assigns a consultant within 24hrs.
  • 4
    Attend Your Appointment Come at your confirmed time with your NIN and reference code. No waiting.
