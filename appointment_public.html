Fill the form below. You'll receive a reference code immediately and SMS confirmation within 24 hours.

No paper, No queue

How Booking Works

Our digital intake system replaces the traditional paper-based process. You do not need to physically travel to Mulago to register for an appointment.

  • 1
    Fill This Form Enter your personal details, NIN, phone number, and reason for visit. All fields marked * are required.
  • 2
    Receive Reference Code You get a unique reference code (e.g. MNR-A8F2K1) on-screen. Screenshot or write it down.
  • 3
    Wait for SMS Confirmation Our admin team reviews your request and sends an SMS with your confirmed date, time, and consultant name within 24 hours.
  • 4
    Attend Your Appointment Arrive 15 minutes early with your National ID Card and reference code. Report to the triage desk at the relevant block.

📋 What to Bring on the Day

  • ✅ National ID Card (NIN)
  • ✅ Your booking reference code
  • ✅ Previous medical records (if any)
  • ✅ Referral letter (if referred from another facility)
  • ✅ Next of kin's contact (for emergency procedures)
🚨 For Emergencies: Do NOT use this form. Go directly to the Accident & Emergency block or call 0800-100-066 (free, 24/7).
