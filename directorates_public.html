Specialized Directorates

Mulago operates 12 specialist institutes across the complex, each with dedicated consultants, operating theatres, and inpatient wards.

❤️

Uganda Heart Institute

Uganda's only public cardiac catheterisation laboratory. Services include interventional cardiology, open-heart surgery, echocardiography, cardiac rehabilitation, and paediatric cardiac care.

Uganda's only public cancer treatment centre. Provides chemotherapy, radiotherapy, bone marrow transplant assessment, palliative care, and psychosocial support. Free services for eligible patients.
